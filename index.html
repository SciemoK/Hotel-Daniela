Our Story

A New Chapter
in a Timeless Place

Nestled in the heart of Bormio — from the Gallic word bormo, meaning "hot spring" — at 1,225 metres above sea level, Hotel Daniela has welcomed guests for generations.

Today, under the passionate stewardship of Antonio Borruso, a Michelin-starred chef, and his wife Antonella, the hotel blends alpine tradition with contemporary warmth. 22 elegantly appointed rooms await you, steps from the old town and Kuerc square.
