Hotel Daniela Bormio

Bormio and Borruso have been inseparable for a long time. As a matter of fact, the charming destination in Alta Valtellina has become the second home of the historic Neapolitan family who has a long tradition in the world of hospitality and gourmet cuisine. So much so that in 2007, at the age of 28, Antonio Borruso obtained his first Michelin star. Today, Antonio – who with his sister Bianca owns Umami Bistrot, where you can enjoy the authentic and inimitable Neapolitan pizza with a gourmet twist – and his wife Antonella have given a second life to Hotel Daniela, one of the oldest and traditional hotels in Valtellina.

Bormio

Bormio – from the Gallic word "bormo", meaning "hot spring" – is situated on a beautiful plain at an altitude of 1,225 m. A real pearl nested between the Alps in Alta Valtellina. Known since ancient times, Bormio or the Magnifica Terra (Magnificent Land) as it is often referred to, is the perfect destination for a holiday devoted to well-being, sports, good food, culture, and... slowing down and living in contact with nature.
